Additional Information

My most significant achievements stem from my years in the band. As a group, we secured several awards for best percussion and have received high ratings in festivals and competitions.

In my personal projects, I've developed productivity programs tailored to streamline and enhance my digital life, focusing on minimalism, automation, and efficiency. Examples of these include file managers, desktop cleaners, and calendar automations.

Among my accomplishments is my acceptance into the Lawrence Berkeley Experiences in Research internship, a highly competitive paid computer science program I undertook the summer before my junior year. This internship marked my first paid computer science job, giving me the amazing opportunity to network with professionals while pursuing my passion of coding.

After that, I pursued other internships, which led to my recent acceptance into the highly competitive high school work-study program with the National Security Agency. Securing this opportunity was a lengthy application process, spanning over a year, making me particularly proud of this achievement. Throughout my senior year of high school, I'll be participating in this program.

Furthermore, I'm on track to graduate with STEM, CTE, and Academic Honors. Looking ahead, I am going to be enrolled in the STEM capstone course next year, in which I aim to undertake an ambitious project. I want to do research related to environmental science, coding, machine learning, math, and engineering. My goal is to develop a physical product with real-world applications that will benefit people in their day-to-day lives.

Ultimately, my interest in the field of computer science is driven by the desire to make a meaningful impact and assist others.
