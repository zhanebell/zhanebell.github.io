About Me

I am a junior at James Campbell High School and a proud member of the S.T.E.A.M. Academy with a passion for computer science, programming, and music. My journey spans three years in the marching band and concert band, while I've also played piano and drums at my church, New Hope Leeward. As a devoted STEAM Academy Ambassador, I am committed to improving our school community. Beyond academics, I enjoy attending chess club, taking on coding projects, and learning new coding languages.

After I graduate high school, my goal is to establish a career with N.S.A. while attending college at my dream school UC Berkeley, in the beautiful state of California. I am on track to graduate in May of 2025 with a total of 5 Advanced Placement Courses, 7 Early College courses, CTE Honors, STEM Honors, Academic Honors, and a 4.2 GPA.
