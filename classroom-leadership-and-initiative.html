Classroom Leadership & Initiative

  • Leadership: While I've never held an official leadership position in my extracurricular activities, I naturally gravitate towards leadership both inside and outside the classroom. In class, I help my peers struggling with concepts, often explaining material when teachers find it challenging to relate with students. I lead group projects by organizing timelines and delegating tasks. Leadership, to me, isn't always formal; anyone can step up and lead. Additionally, at home, as the eldest of four siblings, I often assume responsibility for their care.
  • Intellectual Vitality: My passion for learning is evident in my rigorous coursework, personal projects, internships, and continuous pursuit of knowledge and problem-solving. I consistently enroll in the most challenging AP and early college courses, always striving to stretch my intellectual capabilities. I not only complete these courses but excel in them, dedicating maximum effort to each of them. I further my learning with additional online resources and aim to obtain computer science certifications in my senior year. Through personal projects, I enhance my coding skills and explore new technologies and frameworks. Continuous learning is a big part of my daily routine; I stay updated
